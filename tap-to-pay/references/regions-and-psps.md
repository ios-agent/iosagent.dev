# Countries, Regions, and Supported Payment Service Providers (PSPs)

Tap to Pay on iPhone is available in the following countries and regions. Each entry lists the supported PSPs and, where available, links to their SDK documentation.

# https://developer.apple.com/tap-to-pay/regions/

[View in English](https://developer.apple.com/tap-to-pay/regions/#)

# Countries and regions

Tap to Pay on iPhone is currently available in select countries and regions. To build support for Tap to Pay on iPhone in your app, you’ll first need to integrate with a payment service provider (PSP) who processes the payments read by Tap to Pay on iPhone and provides the certified terminal configurations that are loaded on the merchant’s device. The following PSPs support Tap to Pay on iPhone.
