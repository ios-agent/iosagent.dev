# Marketing Guidelines for Tap to Pay on iPhone

## Apple-Approved Assets and Templates

Apple provides a comprehensive Marketing Guide and Toolkit with the following asset types:

- Display banners
- In-app banners
- Social media tiles
- Lifecycle emails
- Website banners
- Dedicated product pages
- Product animations

Templates allow light customization: brand colors, custom fonts, custom card art, and logos.

Additionally, Apple provides a **Merchant Incentive Guide** for planning merchant incentives.

## Do

- Read the Marketing Guide before starting.
- Use only Apple-approved assets from the Toolkit.
- Only use imagery based on your region.
- Ensure Tap to Pay on iPhone is in full general availability in your app before launching marketing.
- Use the Marketing Guide and Apple-approved assets for all ongoing marketing.
- Leverage the Apple-developed **Merchant Education API** for in-app merchant education.
- Read the Merchant Incentive Guide before planning/launching merchant incentives.
- Submit all PR announcements (press releases, blog posts, investor communications) to Apple for review and approval before publishing. Reviews can take several weeks.

## Don't

- Don't create custom videos, illustrations, photography, or use stock imagery to depict Tap to Pay on iPhone.
- Don't create illustrations or icons depicting iPhone or Tap to Pay on iPhone.
- Don't shorten the name to "Tap to Pay" or include "Apple" â€” always use the full name **"Tap to Pay on iPhone"**.

## Accessing the Toolkit

Prerequisites:
1. Integrate with a supported PSP.
2. Request the entitlement from Apple.

Once assigned an entitlement, the Account Holder of the Apple Developer Program membership receives an email with download instructions for the regional toolkit.
